This project focuses on **exploratory data visualization and storytelling** using a **Superstore-style sales dataset**.  
It demonstrates how data-driven visuals can uncover key business insights and support strategic decisions through a structured report.

---

##  Objectives  
- Perform **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on retail sales data.  
- Create **visualizations** that communicate insights clearly.  
- Build a **storyboard** connecting KPIs, performance trends, and recommendations.  
- Generate a **professional PDF report** summarizing all findings.

---

##  Visuals & Insights  
The PDF report includes the following visualizations and insights:  

1. **Key KPIs Dashboard** ‚Äì Total Sales, Profit, and Avg Order Value  
2. **Sales by Region** ‚Äì Highlights regional performance disparities  
3. **Sales by Category** ‚Äì Reveals top revenue-generating product lines  
4. **Monthly Sales Trend** ‚Äì Tracks seasonal sales fluctuations  
5. **Top 10 Sub-Categories** ‚Äì Identifies best-selling product groups  
6. **Profit vs. Sales** ‚Äì Detects unprofitable high-value orders  
7. **Recommendations & Storyboard** ‚Äì Business actions and strategic insights  

 **Report file:** `Task2_Report.pdf`

---

## Key Insights  
- **West & East regions** contribute the majority of sales.  
- **Office Supplies** leads in revenue, with bundling opportunities in **Technology**.  
- Clear **seasonal peaks** suggest targeted marketing during high-demand months.  
- Some **high-sales orders** show low profit ‚Äî requiring pricing or discount review.
